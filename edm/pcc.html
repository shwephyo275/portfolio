At Parkway Cancer Centre, it’s our mission to understand everything we can about cancer. We’ve seen new complexities but also thankfully, new treatment methodologies. Coupled with the latest technology, we are constantly growing and improving so as to provide quality care for our patients, and this has been so since our inception 15 years ago.

Take example of our haematology team – where the biggest challenge has been to expand from a single haematologist to a team big enough to embrace and develop emerging/innovative therapies. Today, our team’s wide range of skill sets enable us to provide coverage in most fields of haematology, from stem cell transplants to malignant haematology (leukaemia, myeloma and lymphoma) and benign haematology (platelet/ coagulation disorders and anaemias) to lab-based haematology (flowcytometry analysis and stem cell banking).
