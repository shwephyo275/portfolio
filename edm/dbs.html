Automated Financial Analysis tool is coming your way in June!
Automated Financial Analysis a.k.a AutoFIN is a financial analysis tool built by DBS and developed specifically to help RMs analyse financial statements and to generate a first draft of financial analysis write-up for the Credit Memo.
You have been invited to the Beta Launch of AutoFIN!
Three SG IBG 1 segments have been invited for AutoFIN Beta Launch and will be the first to use AutoFIN for financial analysis.
IBG 1 - Shipping, Aviation, Logistics & Transport   IBG 1 - Telecommunication, Media & Technology   IBG 1 - Food & Agribusiness
Come join us in one of the sessions to learn more about AutoFIN and how it can change the way you work!
The training session is mandatory for all RMs, Credit Analysts, CSOs (applicable to the selected 3 IBG 1 segments only) and all IBG 1&2 Credit Risk Managers.
Note: The session is eligible for SFA Supplementary CPD hour.
